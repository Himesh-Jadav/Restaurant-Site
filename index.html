Famous Gujarati Dishes

Khandvi

Thin, delicate rolls made from gram flour and yogurt, spiced with turmeric and garnished with mustard seeds, sesame seeds, and coriander. Khandvi is soft, melt-in-the-mouth, and perfect as a snack.

Check Out
Handvo

A savory baked cake made with a batter of rice, lentils, and vegetables (like bottle gourd). It has a crispy crust and is spiced with sesame seeds and mustard seeds for added flavor.

Check Out
Gujarati Kadhi

A light and tangy yogurt-based curry tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. It is mildly sweet and pairs perfectly with steamed rice or khichdi.

Check Out
Sev Tameta Nu Shaak

A tangy and spicy curry made with tomatoes and topped with crispy sev (fried chickpea flour noodles). It’s best enjoyed with rotis or theplas.

Check Out
Dal Dhokli

A comforting one-pot meal where spiced wheat flour dumplings (dhokli) are cooked in a sweet, tangy, and spicy lentil curry. It’s hearty and wholesome.

Check Out
Patra (Alu Vadi)

Colocasia (arbi) leaves smeared with a spiced gram flour paste, rolled, steamed, and then lightly fried. The dish has a perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and spicy flavors.
